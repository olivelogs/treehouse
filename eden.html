EDEN (Ecological Digital Earth Network)

My pet project, EDEN, is a modular ecosystem model. Inspired by digital twin technology and limited by a macbook air, I find a happy medium between EPA ecoregions and random forests.

EDEN is intended to be a place for both humans and AI to learn something from each other, and in the process, about the world we live in.

She is in active development, open-source, and can be found on my GitHub.
